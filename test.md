# Creative Stone Website

This repository holds the source for the **Creative Stone** company website — a showcase for custom stone work, fabrication, and installation services.

## About Creative Stone

Creative Stone specializes in high-quality stone products and craftsmanship for residential and commercial projects, including:

- Countertops (granite, quartz, marble, and more)
- Custom stone fabrication
- Kitchen and bathroom surfaces
- Fireplace surrounds and accent walls
- Outdoor stone features

## Project Goals

- Present Creative Stone’s portfolio and services clearly to potential customers
- Make it easy to request quotes and get in touch
- Highlight craftsmanship, materials, and completed projects
- Provide a fast, mobile-friendly experience

## Planned Site Structure

| Page        | Purpose                                      |
|-------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Home        | Hero, services overview, featured projects   |
| Services    | Detailed list of offerings                   |
| Gallery     | Photos of completed work                     |
| About       | Company story, team, and values                |
| Contact     | Quote request form, phone, email, location   |
